Pairing Thoughts

May 22, 2014

Our thoughts on pairing from our guided pairing session:

  • Driver Responsibilities: Communication, working through the technical aspects, technical mastery, hands on the keyboard, understanding the navigators vision, applying the pairs ideas
  • Driver Notes: Be clear when speaking up, be open, listen for the navigator, don't worry as much on the big picture, instead focus on the next thing, always be typing, be prepared
  • Navigator Responsibilities: Verbally express the project, hold onto the picture in their head, create a path/plan, monitor the driver, make corrections if necessary
  • Navigator Notes: Keep an eye on the driver's code, think outloud, let the thoughts stream, be honest throughout the process both with yourself and the driver, let the driver know of anything you are looking up
